# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Omnivert adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its Code of Conduct. We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free, and
inclusive experience for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of background
or identity.

## Our standards

In short: be respectful, collaborative, and constructive. We expect community members to act
in ways that contribute to an open and friendly environment, and to refrain from disrespectful
or harassing behavior. The full list of expected and unacceptable behaviors, as well as the
enforcement guidelines and their consequences, is defined by the Contributor Covenant 2.1,
which is incorporated here by reference:

➡️ **https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/**

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions)
and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers
privately. You can:

- Use GitHub's **[private vulnerability/abuse reporting](https://github.com/BrandonDry/Omnivert/security/advisories/new)**, or
- Contact the project maintainer directly via their GitHub profile
  ([@BrandonDry](https://github.com/BrandonDry)).

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated
to respect the privacy and security of anyone who reports an incident.
